Angle Calculator

  • θ = angle of incidence (deg)
  • θm = angle of diffraction of the mth order (deg)
  • λ = wavelength of light (nm)
  • τ = pulse delay time (ps)
  • s = separation between two gratings used to compress a pulse (m)
  • dθm/dλ = angular dispersion, or change in angle of diffraction with wavelength (deg/nm)
  • (1/s)dτ/dλ = pulse dispersion, or change in pulse delay time with wavelength per meter of separation between two identical, parallel gratings (ps/nm per meter)
